About

Chaelin Kim is a graduate design student at Pratt Institute in New York, specializing in interactive media, data visualization, and speculative design.

Her work explores the intersection of technology and social commentary, creating experiences that blend web technologies like Three.js and p5.js with physical fabrication through 3D printing and laser cutting.

With a focus on transformation design and cross-disciplinary approaches, Chaelin creates projects that critique social systems and explore emerging technologies through interactive installations and web-based experiences.
